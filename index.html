Welcome to the Nonprofit Open Data Collective (NPODC). We are a community of practice devoted to the use of open data and development of open source tools in the analysis of non-profit giving and social impact data. You can find us on github, and soon you will find us on social media, through a community blog and twitter.

We welcome contributions and ask to read our standards of conduct. You are also invited to ask for help on how to use any of these resources.
